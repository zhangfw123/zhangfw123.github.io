Fuwei Zhang 张富威

I am a 2rd-year PhD candidate under the supervision of Prof. Fuzhen Zhuang at Institute of Artificial Intelligence, Beihang University. My research focuses on Machine Learning and Data Mining. In particular, I am working on Large Language Model, Information Retrieval, Recommender Systems, and Knowledge Graph Representation.
